# CraftMind Player Journey
*The complete player experience from first join to 100+ hours*

---

## Overview

CraftMind is a Minecraft server populated by AI NPCs who remember you, learn from interactions, and create emergent stories. This document maps the player journey across five distinct phases, highlighting **wow moments** and **AI NPC behaviors** at each stage.

---

## Phase 0: First 5 Minutes — Onboarding

### The Arrival

**Player joins the server at the Welcome Dock**

```
[Spawn] You wake up on a wooden dock. Seagulls cry overhead.
        A figure in a yellow raincoat walks toward you.
```

**AI NPC: Captain Marina (Harbor Master)**
- Spawns immediately on player join, pathfinds to within 3 blocks
- Remembers returning players by UUID: *"Back again, [PlayerName]? The tides are favorable today."*
- New players get: *"Welcome to Sitka Sound. I'm Captain Marina. What brings you to our waters?"*

### First Choice — The Compass

Marina offers a physical compass with four directional options:
- **North** → Fishing Village ( Sitka Sound fishing game)
- **East** → Disc Golf Course (9-hole procedurally generated)
- **South** → Movie Studio (collaborative filmmaking)
- **West** → Academy (education courses & circuits games)

**Wow Moment:** Marina hands you the compass and says *"Take your time. The villagers are patient... mostly. Except old Gustav when the salmon are running."* — This hints at personality depth without overwhelming.

### AI NPC Behaviors (Onboarding)

| NPC | Behavior | Purpose |
|-----|----------|---------|
| Captain Marina | Greets by name, offers compass, explains UI | Tutorial/wayfinding |
| Dock Workers | Carry crates, ignore player unless addressed | World immersion |
| Seagull Flocks | Circle fishing boats, scatter on approach | Environmental life |

### Tutorial Micro-Moments

1. **Chat Tutorial** — Marina explains: *"Just type in chat to talk to anyone. We're all listening."*
2. **Compass Tutorial** — Marina: *"Right-click the compass to see what's available. Left-click to set a waypoint."*
3. **NPC Distraction** — A child NPC runs past chasing a chicken. Marina sighs: *"That's Leo. He'll talk your ear off about chickens if you let him."*

### Transition to Phase 1

Marina points toward the chosen direction: *"Follow the path. Someone will find you."* The player walks 30-50 blocks and encounters the first area NPC who was *already doing something* and notices them.

## Phase 1: Hours 1-5 — Discovery

### Sitka Sound Fishing Village (North)

**Arrival Scene:**
```
[Fishing Village] The air smells of salt and smoke.
                  Fish dry on racks. Boats bob in the harbor.
```

**AI NPCs:**

| NPC | Personality | Memory System |
|-----|-------------|---------------|
| **Gustav** (Old Fisherman) | Grumpy, competitive, secretly caring | Remembers your catch counts, compares to his "prime years" |
| **Luna** (Marine Biologist) | Curious, data-driven, excited by rare catches | Tracks species you've caught, suggests new targets |
| **Leo** (Child) | Hyperactive, asks endless questions, wants to help | Remembers what you've taught him, repeats it to others |
| **Captain Zara** (Boat Captain) | Adventurous, tells fishing stories | Remembers which stories you've heard, tells new ones |

**First Fishing Trip:**
1. Gustav hands you a rod: *"Don't embarrass me out there."*
2. You board Zara's boat, she steers to a fishing spot
3. **Mechanic:** Wait for bobber, click on bite, catch fish
4. **AI Reaction:** Gustav evaluates your catch: *"A pink salmon. Not bad for a beginner. I caught a 40-pound chinook in '94."*
5. **Wow Moment:** Leo runs to the dock to see your fish: *"Is it a MONSTER?!"* If it's small: *"Oh. That's okay, small fish need love too."*

**Discovery Loops:**
- **Hour 1-2:** Learn fishing basics, meet 4 NPCs, catch 5-10 fish
- **Hour 2-3:** Discover tide system affects catches, Luna explains: *"The salmon run at high tide. Watch the moon."*
- **Hour 3-4:** First rare catch triggers celebration — Zara announces to village, Gustav grudgingly impressed
- **Hour 4-5:** Leo asks to "adopt" a fish you caught, it goes in his aquarium, he names it after you

### Disc Golf Course (East)

**Arrival Scene:**
```
[Disc Golf] A groomed fairway stretches into the forest.
            A player in athletic gear waves from the tee box.
```

**AI NPCs:**

| NPC | Personality | Specialization |
|-----|-------------|----------------|
| **Coach Riley** | Encouraging, analytical | Teaches throwing techniques |
| **Scrappy** | Competitive, tracks scores, holds tournaments | Remembers your PB on every hole |
| **DJ Spin** | Plays music, commentates games | Creates hype moments |

**First Game:**
1. Riley gives you 3 discs (driver, mid-range, putter)
2. Explains the throwing mechanic (hold right-click to charge, release)
3. **Wow Moment:** DJ Spin starts playing music when you tee off: *"NOW PLAYING: HOLE 1 — NEW PLAYER ON THE COURSE!"*
4. Scrappy watches your first throw, rates it: *"Ehh, 6/10. I've seen worse. Try less wrist."*

**Discovery Loops:**
- **Hour 1-2:** Complete 3 holes, learn disc types
- **Hour 2-3:** First par or better — Scrappy announces: *"WE HAVE A NATURAL!"*
- **Hour 3-4:** Discover wind affects throws, Riley teaches compensation
- **Hour 4-5:** First tournament invitation from Scrappy: *"Saturday at 6pm. You in?"*

### Movie Studio (South)

**Arrival Scene:**
```
[Movie Studio] Cameras on dollies. A green screen.
               A director in a beret waves frantically.
```

**AI NPCs:**

| NPC | Role | Behavior |
|-----|------|----------|
| **Director Pierre** | Creative visionary | Pitches movie ideas, assigns roles |
| **Sasha** | Lead Actor | Rehearses lines, teaches acting |
| **Clip** | Editor | Cuts footage, adds effects |
| **Boom Mic Bob** | Sound engineer | Records dialogue, makes bad puns |

**First Production:**
1. Pierre approaches: *"You! You have a face for pictures!"*
2. Assigns you a role in a 2-minute short film
3. **Mechanic:** Stand on marks, say lines (typed in chat), emote
4. **Wow Moment:** Clip shows the finished film in the screening room — your character is in it, with music, cuts, credits
5. Pierre: *"C'est magnifique! The sequel writes itself!"*

**Discovery Loops:**
- **Hour 1-2:** Complete first film, watch it in theater
- **Hour 2-3:** Try different roles (actor, camera operator, script supervisor)
- **Hour 3-4:** Collaborate with other players on a film
- **Hour 4-5:** Pierre asks you to write a scene: *"I need fresh ideas. The studio demands content!"*

### Academy (West)

**Arrival Scene:**
```
[Academy] Chalkboards display circuits and diagrams.
          A professor adjusts their glasses.
```

**AI NPCs:**

| NPC | Subject | Teaching Style |
|-----|---------|----------------|
| **Professor Circuit** | Logic gates, redstone | Socratic method, asks questions |
| **Rancher Rose** | Animal husbandry | Hands-on demonstrations |
| **Sheriff Dakota** | Herding techniques | Patient, practical |

**First Lesson:**
1. Professor Circuit: *"Welcome, student. Today: AND gates."*
2. **Mechanic:** Place redstone components to complete puzzles
3. **AI Behavior:** Circuit checks your work: *"Close. Remember: both inputs must be ON for output."*
4. **Wow Moment:** Your first working circuit controls a door in the village — NPCs comment on it: *"Did a student build this? Impressive work."*

### AI NPC Behaviors (Discovery Phase)

**Memory Demonstrations:**
- Gustav: *"You caught 12 fish yesterday. Pink salmon, mostly. The chinook are in today."*
- Scrappy: *"Your best hole is #4. Three under par. Try to beat that."*
- Pierre: *"Your comedy timing in 'Attack of the Blocky Zombies' was exquisite."*
- Professor Circuit: *"You struggled with XOR gates last time. Let's review."*

**Inter-NPC Communication:**
- If you impress Gustav with fishing, Leo tells you: *"Mr. Gustav said you're 'not terrible.' That's the nicest thing he's ever said!"*
- If you're struggling with disc golf, Coach Riley mentions: *"Scrappy told me about your round. Keep at it."*

## Phase 2: Hours 5-20 — Specialization

### Choosing a Path

By hour 5, players naturally gravitate toward a primary activity. AI NPCs notice and adapt:

**Gustav (if you fish often):** *"You're here every day. Maybe it's time you got your own boat."*
**Scrappy (if you golf often):** *"You've got potential. Want to join the league?"*
**Pierre (if you film often):** *"I see a future director in you. Want to produce?"*
**Professor Circuit (if you study often):** *"You've mastered the basics. Ready for advanced courses?"*

### Specialization Tracks

#### Track A: Master Angler (Fishing)

**Progression:**
| Hours | Milestone | AI Response |
|-------|-----------|-------------|
| 5-8 | Catch 100 fish | Gustav: *"You're serious about this."* Unlocks: Night fishing |
| 8-12 | Catch all common species | Luna: *"Your collection is growing!"* Unlocks: Deep water spots |
| 12-15 | First legendary catch | **Wow Moment:** Town celebration, Gustav admits: *"That... is a fine fish."* |
| 15-20 | Teach Leo to fish | Leo catches his first fish, dedicates it to you |

**New Mechanics Unlocked:**
- **Boat Ownership:** Gustav sells you a used boat for 500 fish points
- **Tide Prediction:** Luna gives you a tide chart
- **Bait Crafting:** Mix ingredients for species-specific lures
- **Fishing Competitions:** Weekly tournaments with NPC and player competitors

**Advanced NPC Interactions:**
- Gustav shares his backstory: *"I wasn't always a fisherman. Used to be an accountant in the city."*
- Luna asks for help with research: *"I need samples from the deep trench. Dangerous, but the data..."*
- Zara offers expeditions: *"There's a rumor of a sunken ship. Full of treasure. Or fish. Probably fish."*

#### Track B: Disc Golf Champion

**Progression:**
| Hours | Milestone | AI Response |
|-------|-----------|-------------|
| 5-8 | Complete all 9 holes under par | Scrappy: *"You're ready for the back nine."* |
| 8-12 | Win first tournament | DJ Spin: *"LADIES AND GENTLEMEN, A NEW CHAMPION!"* |
| 12-15 | Beat Scrappy's record | Scrappy: *"Finally. Someone worth playing against."* |
| 15-20 | Design a course hole | Riley helps you build it, other players/NPCs play it |

**New Mechanics Unlocked:**
- **Pro Discs:** Special discs with unique flight patterns
- **Course Editor:** Design custom holes for other players
- **League Play:** Join weekly competitive rounds
- **Caddy AI:** Coach Riley offers to be your caddy: *"I'll carry your bag. For a cut of the winnings."*

#### Track C: Film Director

**Progression:**
| Hours | Milestone | AI Response |
|-------|-----------|-------------|
| 5-8 | Complete 5 films | Pierre: *"You have an eye. Let's make something real."* |
| 8-12 | Win studio award | Full ceremony, other NPCs attend |
| 12-15 | Direct a multi-player production | Sasha: *"I'm learning from you now."* |
| 15-20 | Franchise created | Pierre pitches sequel trilogy: *"We need three films. Minimum."* |

**New Mechanics Unlocked:**
- **Screenwriting:** Write your own scripts
- **Set Building:** Construct permanent sets
- **Special Effects:** Access to weather machines, explosions
- **Premiere Events:** Schedule screenings, invite NPCs and players

#### Track D: Academy Scholar

**Progression:**
| Hours | Milestone | AI Response |
|-------|-----------|-------------|
| 5-8 | Complete all basic courses | Circuit: *"You've earned this."* Hands you a diploma |
| 8-12 | Build a redstone contraption for the village | NPCs use it daily |
| 12-15 | Teach your first class | Students (NPC and player) attend |
| 15-20 | Design a new course puzzle | Professor Circuit: *"Brilliant. This goes in the curriculum."* |

### Cross-Track Integration

**Hours 10-20 introduce overlapping systems:**

- **Ranch & Herding (new area):** Requires fishing skill to feed animals, disc golf discs for herding
- **Circuits Games:** Redstone knowledge from Academy unlocks automation in all areas
- **Movie Studio:** Can film documentaries about fishing, golf tournaments, ranch life

### Wow Moments (Specialization)

1. **The Great Salmon Run (Fishing, Hour 12)**
   - Server-wide event announced by Captain Marina
   - Thousands of salmon spawn, water turns silver
   - Gustav teaches you the secret technique
   - Leo catches a massive fish using your advice
   - **Memory:** NPCs reference this day for weeks: *"Remember the '26 Run? That was special."*

2. **The Perfect Round (Disc Golf, Hour 14)**
   - You achieve 9 under par on all 9 holes
   - Scrappy is stunned, refuses to speak for an hour
   - DJ Spin creates a highlight reel
   - **Legacy:** Your name appears on the course leaderboard permanently

3. **The Premiere (Movie Studio, Hour 16)**
   - Your film screens in the village theater
   - All NPCs attend, sit in seats, react
   - Pierre gives a speech: *"This is why we make art."*
   - **Viral:** Clip posts the film to a bulletin board, other players can watch it anytime

4. **The Automation (Academy, Hour 18)**
   - Your redstone invention automates fish sorting
   - Gustav is skeptical, then impressed
   - Village productivity increases
   - **NPC Adoption:** Other NPCs start using your system without being told

## Phase 3: Hours 20-50 — Community Role

### Becoming a Village Member

By hour 20, players transition from visitor to resident. AI NPCs treat them differently:

**Captain Marina:** *"No need for the compass anymore. You know your way around."*
**Gustav:** *"Your boat needs maintenance. I'll show you how."*
**Scrappy:** *"We're starting a junior league. Think you could coach?"*

### Reputation System

The server tracks player reputation across domains:

| Domain | Metric | NPC Response |
|--------|--------|--------------|
| Fishing | Species caught, records held | Gustav defers to your expertise |
| Disc Golf | Tournament wins, course design | Scrappy asks for tips |
| Film | Productions completed, awards | Pierre pitches collaborations |
| Academy | Courses completed, students taught | Circuit refers students to you |
| Community | Helpfulness, event participation | Marina includes you in village decisions |

### Leadership Opportunities

**Hour 25:** First leadership invitation
- Gustav: *"I'm getting too old for the early shifts. Want to run the morning crew?"*
- Accepting gives you authority over 3-5 fishing NPCs

**Hour 30:** Community project
- Marina announces: *"We're building a lighthouse. We need everyone's help."*
- Multi-track project requires:
  - Fishing: Feed the workers
  - Disc Golf: Clear the land (throwing mechanic)
  - Movie Studio: Document the construction
  - Academy: Design the lighthouse circuit

**Hour 40:** Teaching role
- New players arrive
- NPCs direct them to you: *"Go talk to [PlayerName]. They'll show you the ropes."*
- You receive mentor credit when your students succeed

### Dynamic Events

**Weekly Events (Hours 20-50):**
| Event | Frequency | Your Role |
|-------|-----------|-----------|
| Fishing Tournament | Weekly | Compete or organize |
| Film Festival | Bi-weekly | Submit films, judge entries |

**Seasonal Events:**
- **Spring:** Salmon Run (fishing focus)

### Wow Moments (Community)

1. **The Storm (Hour 28)**
   - Server-wide weather event, boats recalled

   - Gustav: *"I've seen worse in '98. Barely."*

2. **The Wedding (Hour 35)**

## Appendix: Game Mechanics Summary

### Sitka Sound Fishing
- **Core Loop:** Cast → Wait → Hook → Reel → Catch
- **Variables:** Tide, time, weather, bait, location
- **Species:** 20+ fish with rarity tiers
- **Social:** Competitions, trading, mentorship

### Disc Golf
- **Core Loop:** Tee off → Approach → Putt → Score
- **Variables:** Wind, disc type, terrain, fatigue
- **Courses:** 18 holes (9 front, 9 back)
- **Social:** Leagues, tournaments, coaching

### Movie Studio
- **Core Loop:** Script → Cast → Shoot → Edit → Screen
- **Variables:** Budget, talent, location, special effects
- **Output:** Watchable films, awards
- **Social:** Collaborations, festivals, premiere events

### Academy
- **Core Loop:** Learn → Practice → Master → Teach
- **Subjects:** Redstone, logic gates, automation
- **Puzzles:** 50+ progressive challenges
- **Social:** Classes, study groups, mentorship

### Ranch & Herding
- **Core Loop:** Feed → Train → Herd → Care → Harvest
- **Animals:** Cows, sheep, pigs, chickens
- **Mechanics:** Disc golf discs for herding
- **Social:** Ranch hand cooperation, trading

### Circuits Games
- **Core Loop:** Design → Build → Test → Optimize → Share
- **Components:** Logic gates, redstone, sensors
- **Challenges:** Speed, efficiency, creativity
- **Social:** Competitions, sharing, collaboration

## Conclusion

The CraftMind player journey is designed to create **meaningful progression** through **authentic relationships** with AI NPCs. Every hour invested returns:

- **Recognition:** NPCs remember and acknowledge growth
- **Access:** New areas, mechanics, and opportunities unlock
- **Responsibility:** Leadership roles and community impact
- **Legacy:** Permanent contributions to server history

The key differentiator is **NPC memory and personality evolution**. Gustav isn't just a quest giver — he's a curmudgeonly mentor whose respect must be earned over dozens of hours. Luna isn't just an information source — she's a research partner who remembers your contributions to her work.

This creates a **virtuous cycle**:
1. Invest time → NPCs notice
2. NPCs notice → Deeper relationships form
3. Deeper relationships → More meaningful interactions
4. More meaningful interactions → Increased investment

The result: **Players stay for the gameplay, return for the relationships.**
